# The Haunting - Horror Game 👻

A browser-based horror game built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

## 🎮 Play Now

Visit: **https://therealbutters1-eng.github.io**

## 📖 How to Play

### Controls
- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** - Move your character
- **SHIFT** - Run (use cautiously)
- **E** - Interact with items

### Objective
1. Survive the night in an abandoned mansion
2. Collect the golden **KEY** from the far corner
3. Reach the **EXIT** at the bottom-right corner
4. Avoid shadow entities and manage your health & sanity

### Game Mechanics
- **Health**: Decreases when enemies catch you. Collect health potions to restore.
- **Sanity**: Decreases in darkness, restored in light. Keep it above 0 to survive.
- **Enemies**: Three shadow entities that hunt you through the mansion.
- **Lights**: Safe zones that help restore your sanity.
- **Items**: Keys and health potions scattered throughout.

## ✨ Features

✨ Retro green screen aesthetic with dynamic darkness overlay
✨ Enemy AI with chase mechanics
✨ Collision detection and physics
✨ Multiple game states (start, play, game over, win)

## 🎮 Tips to Survive

1. **Stay in the light** - Your sanity will thank you
2. **Avoid the shadows** - They're faster than you might think
3. **Collect supplies** - Health potions are your friend
4. **Find the key** - Can't escape without it
5. **Run strategically** - Use walls to your advantage

## 💀 Game Over Conditions

- **Health reaches 0** - Enemies caught you
- **Sanity reaches 0** - You lost your mind

## 🏆 Winning

Collect the key and reach the exit corner while keeping both health and sanity above 0.
