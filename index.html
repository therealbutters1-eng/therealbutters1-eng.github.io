THE HAUNTING

A Horror Experience

You wake up in an abandoned mansion with no memory of how you got here. Strange shadows lurk in the darkness. Survive the night.

HOW TO PLAY

Movement: Arrow Keys or WASD

Interact: E key

Run: SHIFT (drains stamina)

Objective: Find the exit key and escape before dawn

Avoid: The Shadow entities that roam the halls

Stay Safe: Turn on lights and hide in dark corners

GAME OVER

YOU ESCAPED!

Congratulations! You made it out alive!
